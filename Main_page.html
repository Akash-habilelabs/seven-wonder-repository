Types of Cars in India: Car Body Types Explained

The journey towards truly falling in love with cars or even owning one starts with knowing the fundamentals. When you decide to buy a car, the first thing you might want to know is the type of car that will best serve your requirement. Having reasonable knowledge about the different types of cars the Indian car market has to offer will help set you on the right path to your dream car.

To help you on your journey, we bring you a comprehensive guide to understanding car body types in India, along with the popular cars in each category.
